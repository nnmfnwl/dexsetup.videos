Next is to configure Dex-Bot the decentralized exchange automatic liquidity and trading bot.

Dex-setup installer contains predefined trading strategy configurations which are applied on predefined dexbot default template to generate trading strategy configuration and start scripts. Trading pairs are for now configure by default with Litecoin.

This process could take some few seconds, as it is searching for all wallets configuration files to update it according to blocknet decentralized exchange system compatibility.

Remember this is just very basic automatic trading bot configuration. It is advanced and tested but still considered experimental, so it is recommended to use just very small amounts of coins. Expected is to have many different trading startegy configurations per trading pairs based on market conditions. For example when market or trading pair gets volatile to both sides or even just to downtrend, it depends if you are providing liquitiy to save your business or you are going on profit to exist market fast.
